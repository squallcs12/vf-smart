1. Overview

VF Smart ("the app") is a companion application for the VF Smart ESP32 hardware module. It connects to a device on your local WiFi network to control and monitor your VinFast VF3 electric vehicle.

Short version All data stays on your device or on your local network. We do not operate any servers, collect analytics, or sell data. The only external network contact the app makes is with Google Play for subscription management.

2. Data we collect

The app collects only what is necessary to function:

3. Android permissions explained

INTERNET & ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE

Required to communicate with the ESP32 device over your local WiFi network via HTTP and WebSocket.

ACCESS_WIFI_STATE & CHANGE_WIFI_MULTICAST_STATE

Used for UDP broadcast discovery (port 8888) to automatically find the ESP32 on your network without manual IP entry.

ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION & ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION

Optionally used to search for nearby EV charging stations. Location is sent to the charging-station lookup service only when you actively use that feature and is never stored or logged by the app.

BIND_NOTIFICATION_LISTENER_SERVICE

Allows the app to read notifications from Google Maps while navigation is active. Only notifications from com.google.android.apps.maps are read, and only to extract the current maneuver instruction (e.g. "Turn left in 300 m") for display on the ODO screen. No notification content is stored or transmitted.

How to grant / revoke Settings → Apps → Special app access → Notification access → VF Smart. The ODO navigation feature simply shows "NO NAVIGATION" if this permission is not granted.

com.android.vending.BILLING

Required for the Google Play in-app subscription. See Third-party services.

4. Third-party services

Google Play Billing

The app uses Google Play's billing system to manage the Premium subscription. When you subscribe, Google processes the payment and provides the app with a purchase token. The app uses that token solely to verify subscription status locally. Google's handling of your payment information is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Google Assistant / App Actions

Voice commands ("Hey Google, lock my car") are processed entirely by Google Assistant on your device. The app receives a deep-link intent from the Assistant and executes the corresponding action on the local ESP32. No voice data is received or stored by the app.

Android Auto

The Android Auto integration displays car status and control buttons on a car's head unit screen. No additional data is collected beyond what the main app already uses.

The app contains no advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or crash-reporting SDKs.

5. How data is stored

Sensitive configuration (device IP, API key, device name, MAC address) is stored using Android's EncryptedSharedPreferences with AES-256-GCM encryption, backed by the Android Keystore. This data is accessible only to the app and is not included in device backups.

Non-sensitive data (first launch timestamp) is stored in standard SharedPreferences on-device.

No data is stored in the cloud, on remote servers, or in any location outside your device.

6. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party, except as described in Section 4 (Google Play Billing, governed by Google's own privacy policy).

API commands (e.g. lock, unlock) are sent to the ESP32 device on your local network. They never leave your network and are not relayed through any intermediary server.

7. Children's privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Your rights

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times:

  • Access: All stored data is visible through the app's Settings screen.
  • Delete: Use Settings → Clear device configuration in the app, or uninstall the app, to erase all locally stored data.
  • Subscription: Manage or cancel your subscription at any time through the Google Play Store → Subscriptions.
  • Location: Revoke location permission at any time in Android Settings → Apps → VF Smart → Permissions.
  • Notification access: Revoke at any time in Android Settings → Apps → Special app access → Notification access.
